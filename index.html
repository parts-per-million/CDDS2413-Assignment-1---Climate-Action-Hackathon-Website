Climate Action Hackathon 2025 - Urban Heat & Flood Resilience

November 7th, 2025

About

Purpose

The Climate Action Hackathon is a yearly innovation event focused on addressing growing climate challenges around the world. This hackathon brings together students, developers, designers and community innovators to co-create digital solutions that will help communities deal with the problems of climate change.

This year's theme concerns urban areas, specifically on how to combat rising temperatures and frequent flash floods.

Goals

The ultimate goal of the Climate Action Hackathon is the transformation of climate data and ideas into practical, community-impactful digital products, encouraging innovation and environmental responsibility.

Expected Outcome

By the end of this hackathon, we should expect many digital creations that will contribute to helping urban communities. These can include:

  • Green infrastructure mapping, smart shading prototypes, or cooling strategies which will mitigate increasing urban heat.
  • Early-warning systems, community response tools, or interactive dashboards that improve flood resilience and emergency services.
  • Data visualization, storytelling, or geospatial tools raising climate risk awareness in urban areas.

Connection to SDG

The hackathon supports "SDG 13: Climate Action", which is part of the UN's Sustainable Development Goals inititative. We want to help solve the problems of climate change, and we believe the best path lies with technology. This gathering of all minds to make more solutions will bring us one step closer to solving climate change for good.
